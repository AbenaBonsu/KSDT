KSDT

FIERCELY INDEPENDENT STUDENT RADIO

We are an independent student-run online radio station at UCSD. With over 100 student DJ's, we believe in sharing diverse tastes in music and unique perspectives.

History

KSDT is a student-run radio station located on the campus of the University of California, San Diego. The station has existed since 1967, but has taken many different forms since then. In its current configuration, KSDT broadcasts solely via streaming MP3 on the internet.
